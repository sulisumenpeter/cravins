Our Story

Founded in 2015, Cravins Ice Cream began as a small family-owned gelato cart in Lagos. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved local institution known for our innovative flavors and commitment to quality.

We source our ingredients from local Nigerian farmers and producers whenever possible, ensuring the freshest, most flavorful results in every scoop.
